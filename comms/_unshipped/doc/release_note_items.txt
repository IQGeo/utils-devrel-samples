GUI
---
   - [Isaac] Circuits can only be added to fiber cables. Circuits cannot be added to exclusively copper or coaxial equipment. Circuits can be added to the fiber side of mixed technology equipment.

   - Trees now support textual filtering
   - Cable connection trees also support filtering by containment
   - Structure cable trees support new context menu item 'Set Enclosure' on cable segments
   - Adding equipment from equipment tree context menu now has technology sub-menus (if > 1 technology is configured in the system)
   
   - Line of count [Mark]
        - Edit and view line of count option available from cable and equipment context menu.
        - Line of count displayed against fiber, pair and port in tree view. Toggle on/off
