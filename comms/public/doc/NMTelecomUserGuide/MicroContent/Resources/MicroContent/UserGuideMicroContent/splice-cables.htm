To connect two cables:

  1. Select the structure, such as a manhole, where the splice will be made. You can either use the Search box or select the object on the map.
  2. In the Equipment tree, find the object in which the splice will be made. Note that you may need to add a splice closure or similar housing.

  3. Right-click the equipment, and then click Connect Cables.

    The Connect Cables dialog appears listing the cables that are present in the structure.

    Figure: Connect Cables selection dialog

    If the list of cables is long, you can use the filtering feature at the top of the dialog, both for the From side and the To side. By entering (a part of the name) you can shorten the list to only those items that are of interest to you.
    Yet another way of shortening the list of cables is by selecting the appropriate containing equipment at the bottom of the dialog. This reduces the list of cables to only those contained in the selected structure.

  4. To connect the cables:

    Figure: Connect Cables dialog

    1. In the From column, select one or more strands that you want to connect. To select multiple strands, press the Shift key and click the required strands.

    2. In the To column, click the strands to connect to.

    3. Click Connect.

    The new splice appears as an entry in the Equipment tree. You can also view connections from the Cable tree.

    Figure: Splicing

Notes:

  • When you hover on the details, the connected objects are highlighted on the map.
  • You can move a splice to a different housing in the structure by dragging and dropping it.
  • If you are making many splices, click the Pin icon in the Connect Cables dialog.
